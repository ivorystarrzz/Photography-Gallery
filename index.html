About Me


Freelance Photographer


My name is Ivory Starr and I am a freelance photographer with a focus on portraits. Born and raised in Indianapolis, Indiana, I started my journey into photography over 10 years ago. My favorite subject to focus on are people. I love to meet new people and capture their genuine moments. From silly family portraits to silly maternity photos, good photography has the ability to hold that moment in time.
